A little bit more than a week ago I posted a blog post where I discussed a project that makes Disqus much faster. At the time the new version was available only for our early adopters and they did a great job by testing it and sending us their feedback. Since then, we’ve fixed a few issues and made some more improvements. And today I am happy to announce that we made this faster, more responsive version available to everybody!

You don’t need to do anything to enable it but if you experience any difficulties loading Disqus, there is a new link under the progress bar. By clicking on it, you can send an automatic bug report to us and (if you are a moderator) temporarily disable the update for your site (until we fix an issue).

-Anton
